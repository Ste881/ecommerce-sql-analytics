# Business Insights

## Key Findings

1. A small group of customers contributes to multiple purchases, indicating repeat buying behavior.
2. Revenue is distributed across multiple months, showing consistent activity.
3. Certain products have higher sales volume, indicating strong demand.
4. Customer segmentation reveals differences in purchasing frequency and spending.

## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing on repeat customers
- Promote high-performing products
- Improve retention strategies for low-frequency users

## Advanced Insights

- Customer retention varies across cohorts, indicating differences in engagement.
- A small subset of customers ranks highest in spending, highlighting high-value users.
- Revenue shows cumulative growth over time, indicating steady business performance.
